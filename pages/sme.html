03 — SME & Corporate Solutions

Business solutions tailored to your needs, no matter your size.

As a business owner, you face competing demands: growing your company, retaining talented employees, protecting your assets and planning for continuity. The Afken Advisory Services manages your business and personal finances as two parts of one integrated whole.

Corporate

Employee Benefits

Have you reviewed your company's benefits package lately? It's one of the best culture-defining, recruiting and retention tools you'll ever have. Your employees are your business's biggest asset — providing benefits recognises their value and makes their futures more secure.

  • Group life, disability and funeral cover scheme design and provider selection
  • Group retirement annuity / provident fund structuring and ongoing administration
  • Annual scheme benchmarking against market alternatives
  • Staff communication support so employees actually understand their benefits
Corporate

Keyman Cover

Many employers in small to medium-sized businesses are great at looking after everyone except themselves. Keyman cover protects the business against the loss of a key person whose absence would significantly impact revenue or operations.

  • Valuation-based cover sizing tied to the keyperson's actual contribution to revenue or operations
  • Policy structuring to ensure proceeds are paid to the business, not the individual's estate
  • Annual reviews as the business grows or key roles change
Corporate

Buy-and-Sell Agreements

Without the correct corporate structures and agreements, the ownership interests of a business can be disrupted on the death or disability of a partner. A properly funded buy-and-sell agreement ensures continuity and eliminates the need to sell assets to meet obligations.

  • Drafting of buy-and-sell agreements aligned to your shareholders' agreement
  • Life and disability cover funding structured to match each partner's share value
  • Regular share valuation reviews to keep funding accurate as the business grows
Corporate

Succession Planning

Many owners trade as sole proprietors or in partnerships without guaranteeing that the benefits of their business interests will flow to the right people. The correct corporate structures, owned by trusts, coupled with the necessary agreements must be implemented to ensure continuity.

  • Ownership transition structuring for family businesses and management buyouts
  • Integration with personal estate and retirement planning for exiting owners
  • Funding mechanisms for gradual or lump-sum ownership transfers
Corporate

Personal Financial Planning for Owners

The Afken Advisory Services range extends to assuring the business owner's personal finances — covering risk events, key persons, and making sure the owner's own retirement goals are met alongside the business obligations they carry every day.

  • Business interruption cover sized against realistic downtime scenarios
  • Professional indemnity and public liability tailored to your sector
  • Office, equipment and stock cover with annual revaluation
Case Study

Funding a buy-and-sell agreement for a three-partner practice

Three equal partners in a professional services firm had a shareholders' agreement but no funded mechanism behind it — meaning a partner's death would have forced the others to either find cash fast or accept a new, unplanned co-owner.

We valued each partner's share, structured matched life cover to fund a buyout at fair value, and built the agreement so a transition — should it happen — is automatic and pre-funded.

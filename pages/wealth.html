01 — Wealth Management

A coordinated plan for growing and transferring wealth.

Investments, retirement, trusts, estate planning, wills, education funding and preservation — built as one connected strategy, not seven separate products.

Investments

Local & Global Investments

Diversification isn't a buzzword on our side of the desk — it's the difference between a portfolio that survives a downturn and one that doesn't. We build portfolios across local and offshore markets, matched to your risk profile, time horizon and currency exposure.

  • Local unit trusts, ETFs and direct equity portfolios across major asset managers
  • Offshore investment structuring for rand-hedge and currency diversification
  • Tax-free savings accounts and discretionary investment wrappers
  • Quarterly performance reviews benchmarked against your original plan, not just the market
Retirement

Retirement Plan

Retirement planning is really two separate problems: building enough capital, and then turning that capital into an income that lasts. We model both from day one, so the number you're saving toward actually means something.

  • Retirement annuities and pension/provident fund structuring
  • Living annuity and life annuity comparisons at retirement
  • Income-in-retirement modelling, stress-tested against inflation and longevity
  • Tax-efficient contribution structuring within annual allowances
Structuring

Trusts

A trust is a tool, not a tax shelter — used well, it protects assets for the people you intend to benefit, across generations, and outside of the delays and costs of an estate.

  • Inter-vivos (living) and testamentary trust establishment
  • Trustee appointment guidance and governance structuring
  • Asset transfer planning into existing or new trust structures
  • Annual trust administration and compliance coordination with your accountant
Legacy

Estate Planning

Without a plan, your estate is settled by formula, not by your intentions — and the costs (executor fees, estate duty, liquidity shortfalls) land on the people you meant to protect. We plan for all three in advance.

  • Estate liquidity analysis — does your estate have cash to settle its own costs?
  • Estate duty and capital gains tax minimisation strategies
  • Coordination between your will, trusts and beneficiary nominations so nothing contradicts
  • Executor selection guidance and fee benchmarking
Foundations

Wills

More than 60% of South African adults don't have an up-to-date will. Without one, the Intestate Succession Act decides who inherits — not you. Drafting or updating yours is often the single highest-impact thing we do for a new client.

  • Will drafting and safe custody, included as part of an ongoing advisory relationship
  • Guardian nomination guidance for minor children
  • Reviews triggered automatically after marriage, divorce, birth or major asset changes
  • Coordination with trust and estate structures to avoid conflicting instructions
Future Planning

Education Plans

Education costs compound faster than most other household expenses. We build dedicated, ring-fenced investment plans for school and tertiary fees, so a market dip doesn't become a missed school term.

  • Fee-inflation-adjusted projections per child, per institution type
  • Dedicated investment vehicles separate from retirement and general savings
  • Flexible contribution structures for variable income earners and business owners
Continuity

Preservation Funds

Changing jobs shouldn't mean cashing out your retirement savings and starting from zero. Preservation funds keep that capital working, tax-efficiently, until you actually retire.

  • Pension and provident preservation fund setup on resignation or retrenchment
  • One-off withdrawal guidance — used wisely, since it's a once-off option per fund
  • Investment strategy review on transfer, rather than a default fund choice
Case Study

Consolidating a fragmented retirement portfolio for a business-owning couple

Two preservation funds, an old retirement annuity, and a discretionary portfolio sitting across three providers, with no shared strategy. We consolidated into a single coordinated plan, restructured for tax efficiency, and aligned the couple's retirement date with their business succession timeline.

The result wasn't just tidier paperwork — it was a clearer, lower-cost path to the income they'll actually need.

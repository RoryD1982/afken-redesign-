Medical Aid Comparisons

Medical aid plans vary enormously in what they actually cover and cost. We compare options across major South African medical schemes against your real usage patterns and budget — not just the premium price — so you end up on the right plan, not just a cheaper one.

  • Side-by-side comparison across major South African medical schemes
  • Plan-matching based on actual claims history, not just premium cost
  • Annual scheme review during the medical aid open enrolment period
  • Support navigating waiting periods, late-joiner penalties and underwriting
Healthcare

Gap Cover

Medical aid rarely covers 100% of specialist and in-hospital costs. Gap cover closes the shortfall between what your scheme pays and what providers actually charge — often the difference between a manageable bill and a financial shock.

  • Shortfall cover for in-hospital specialist and procedure costs
  • Plan selection matched to your medical aid's specific reimbursement rates
  • Particularly relevant for chronic conditions and planned procedures

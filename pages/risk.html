02 — Risk Management

Protection built around what you actually can't afford to lose.

Income, health, dependants, and the assets you've spent years building — each needs a different kind of protection. We structure cover so it actually pays out when it matters, not just when it's convenient.

Life Cover Disability Severe Illness Income Protection Specialist Assets
Protection

Life Cover

Life cover exists to replace what your income and presence provide — debt settlement, ongoing household costs, and your family's plans staying intact. We size it to the actual gap, not a rule of thumb.

  • Needs-based cover calculation: debt, income replacement, and dependants' future costs
  • Beneficiary nomination structuring to avoid estate delays
  • Policy reviews triggered by bond changes, new dependants, or business growth
  • Co-ordination with your will and estate plan so payouts go where intended

Disability Cover

A disability that prevents you from working is statistically far more likely than an early death — yet it's the cover most people underinsure. We make sure lump-sum and income-replacement disability cover both feature in your plan.

  • Lump-sum disability cover for major capital needs — home modifications, debt settlement
  • Own-occupation vs any-occupation definitions explained in plain terms before you sign
  • Coordination with income protection so there's no gap between policies
  • Regular occupation and income reviews to keep cover sized correctly

Severe Illness Cover

A cancer, stroke or heart attack diagnosis brings costs medical aid won't fully cover — and an income gap while you recover. Severe illness cover pays a lump sum directly to you, to use as needed.

  • Condition-specific payout structuring across major illness categories
  • Integration with medical aid and gap cover to close out-of-pocket gaps
  • Family history and occupational risk factored into cover recommendations

Protection

Income Protection

If you're temporarily unable to work, income protection replaces a portion of your salary monthly — covering the gap that lump-sum cover alone doesn't address.

  • Monthly income replacement structured against your actual take-home pay
  • Waiting period and benefit term selection matched to your savings buffer
  • Particularly critical for self-employed clients and commission-based earners

Specialist Asset Cover

Standard household policies often under-cover or simply exclude high-value vehicles, pleasure craft, art and private collections. We source specialist cover that actually understands what you own.

  • Agreed-value cover for classic, performance and high-value vehicles
  • Pleasure craft and marine cover, including liability extensions
  • Fine art, jewellery and private collection valuation-based cover
  • Annual revaluation so cover keeps pace with appreciating assets

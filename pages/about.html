About Afken Advisory Services

At Afken, we believe financial planning is about more than products and services.

What sets us apart is our deep desire to care. Only by caring passionately about our clients can we understand what drives them, what they want their legacy to be, and what they want from their relationship with us.

Our Story

Established 1986. Still keeping things personal.

Afken Advisory Services was established in 1986 with a commitment to giving sound personal and business financial planning advice. We continue that commitment today — with a national footprint across South Africa and over R2.5 billion in funds under management.

Our clients are as diverse as the markets in which they live, invest and do business. We treat them as we would like to be treated ourselves — with honesty, respect, intelligence and understanding. We take a holistic, individual approach that requires highly personalised service.

Today, trust is the most rare commodity. Our clients turn to us for reassurance, support and guidance. Our team are part confidante and full-time advisors — covering both financial planning solutions and short-term insurance for individuals and businesses.

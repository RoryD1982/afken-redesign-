About Afken Global Wealth

Independent advice, built for the long run.

Afken Global Wealth was founded on a simple premise: that financial advice works best when it isn't tied to a single product shelf. We're independent, product-agnostic, and structured to stay accountable to one thing — your plan actually working.

Our Story

A national practice, built on local relationships

Afken Global Wealth advises individuals, families and business owners across South Africa, with a footprint that spans major centres while keeping every client relationship personally managed by a dedicated advisor.

Over more than two decades, that approach has scaled into a practice managing advice on over R2.5 billion in client assets — without losing the thing that built it: advisors who actually know their clients' full financial picture, not just a single policy number.

We work across five disciplines — wealth, risk, business, health, and short-term insurance — because in practice, none of those decisions happen in isolation. A business sale affects an estate plan. A new dependant changes a risk profile. We plan for the whole picture, deliberately.
